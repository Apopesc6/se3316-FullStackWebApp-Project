DMCA Workflow:

On the manager page, click the button that says "Log Requests, Notices, and Disputes" to access the tools available. You are then granted the ability to log either a request, notice or dispute of your choice which is stored in a database and later displayed on the screen after it is saved.


DMCA Tools Instructions:

  1. Enter your request/notice/dispute in the appropriate box.
  2. Click save to save the entry in the database.
  3. If done correctly, it will be saved in the database.
  4. Momentarily, the page will refresh and show your new entry on the screen under the appropriate title.
